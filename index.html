About Me

Hello! My name is Ginea Merrill and welcome to my Web Development Portfolio!

I am passionate about web development and design. I am a quick learner and I work well alone and with others. Computers and technology has long been a part of my life and I could not imagine a world without them in it. I am starting my career as a Web Developer, with the help of the Eleven Fifty Academy, and while I would describe my head-long plunge into the field as one of the hardest things I have done, I would also describe it as one of the most rewarding experiences in my life. I find that I thrive and grow exponentially under pressure and look forward to continuing my growth within this field.

Before this career change, I worked at Best Buy in their Digital Imaging department which grew my passion for photography and is one of the reasons I started and continue to use the Adobe suite of programs for photography and design. While at Best Buy, I crossed trained in their Home Theater department, their Computer department, and then their Portable Electronics department.

After this, I did some traveling and was Nanny to a couple of families which, while a departure from my previous and current life, was fulfilling and rewarding in its own right. I later found a new career at Plaquemaker Plus. I started on the production line, moved to typesetting, then visual design, and ultimately graphic design and trainer. I left to focus on higher education which is when Covid-19 hit. In the midst of the pandemic is when I discovered the Eleven Fifty Academy and made this new change for the better. As I look for my best fit with a company in Web Development, I am working again at Plaquemaker Plus as Full-Color Sublimation Lead.

Awards and Badges

Gold Badge

The earners of Web Development Gold Badge have learned the fundamentals of front-end web design with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Students pull data from APIs, manipulate the DOM, create repositories in Github, and begin to deploy webpages.

Blue Badge

The earners of the Web Development Blue Badge learn how to build faster more performant front ends with React and React capable libraries.
