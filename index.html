About Me

Hello! My name is Ginea Merrill and Welcome to my web developement portfolio!

During college, I worked at Best Buy in their camera department and really grew my passion for photography. I later found a new career in plaque making at Plaquemaker Plus. I started on the production line, moved to typsetting, then visual design, and ultimately graphic design.

Now, I am starting my career as a Web Developer with the help of the Eleven Fifty Academy. Going from Graphic Designer to Web Developer has become a natural step for me, and I am excited for this new career path and for all the places this choice will take me.
